Nail Salon Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to give information and schedule services for the customers of this nail salon. Customers will be able to see the services that are offered, find contact information, as well as book services with the salon.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website is going to be mainly women from ages 16-70 (mainly women because that is usually who gets nails done). This website will be for women who live in Rexburg, who like to get their nails done, and who want efficient ways to be able to schedule getting appointments with the salon.

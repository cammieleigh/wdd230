Target Audience

The target audience for this website is people that are living in Lehi that want to better understand the businesses that are in the city. These can be people that are interested in joining the chamber of commerce or are looking to better understand their city.

Scenarios

Grace is a local business owner who looking to get more support for her business. She can use the chamber of commerce sign to become a verified business in the community and also connect with other local business owners.

Paul is a man that recently moved in to the area. He left a lot of businesses and people that he really liked in his old town, and is looking for places to run errends, such as grocrey shopping and getting his haircut. He can use the chamber site to find businesses that look suitable to his needs.

Attractions and Features

Thanksgiving Point

Thanksgiving Point is a great place for kids and adults alike! There are museums, beautiful gardens, and even a butterfly biosphere. Come for a night out, or a fun Saturday activity!

Hutchings Museum

This museum is a fun stop to see the collection of John Hutchings and much more! It holds exhibits including found pieces of Native American culture, as well as rocks and minerals and even more.

Thanksgiving Point Golf Course

The Thanksgiving Point golf course is a great course that offers an awesome day on the green.

Megaplex Theater

The Megaplex movie theater is found in a bustling part of Lehi with lots to do! The theater features all of the latest movies, and offers great food to snack on while you enjoy the show!

Ivory Ridge Splashpad

The splashpad park in Lehi is a fun park that includes a playground, a large grassy area, and a splashpad that kids love in the Summer!

American Fork Canyon

The American Fork canyon is a short drive away from Lehi. It draws visitors in with beautiful hikes, nice campgrounds, and its many bodies of water.

Lehi Rollermills

Not only does the Lehi rollermill offer baked goods, they also offer dry mixes to take home and bake. See one of the locations for the original Footloose and enjoy a sweet treat!

Traverse Mountain Outlet Mall

The outlet mall at Traverse Mountain offers a wide variety of shops, that cater to every person. Enjoy a pretzal while you're looking around!

Momentum

The Momentum climbing gym in Lehi offers high-quality bouldering for all skill levels of climbers. From V0 to V14 any climber can find a good challenge!
